About the Beat

We use Instagram pictures users added to their map, and combine them with the corresponding Google StreetView location to help explore topics in a new way. Try it!

This is a project of the Social Media Information Lab, at the School of Communications and Information at Rutgers University.

Feel free to explore your own beat by putting a new topic in the search box.

Read more about the lab at sm.rutgers.edu or Follow @sminfolab
